Welcome to Guild XoX 🎉

We’re excited to have you join Guild XoX in Hero Wars! Please review the following guidelines to ensure smooth teamwork and enjoyable gameplay.

These guidelines cover various aspects of guild activities, including guild wars, raids, arenas, adventures, special events, and general conduct, to help you navigate guild life effectively.

1. Guild War

Our guild currently operates Guild War using a free attack format. Based on solid defensive strength, members are free to choose their targets and attack at their preferred time using their own judgment. However, this doesn’t mean we encourage completely random target selection. We do provide strategic guidelines to help with target prioritization.

  • Capture priority: Bridge → One of the three gates → Citadel → Remaining fortifications
  • Recommended target order based on capture bonuses:
    • +120 bonus: Citadel
    • +60 bonus: Bridge, Gates of Nature, Bastion of Fire, Bastion of Ice, Spring of Elements, Foundry
    • +40 bonus: Barracks, Mage Academy, Lighthouse
  • Once we reach the endgame with over 700 capture points, we should focus on easier targets to maximize additional capture bonuses.
  • IMPORTANT! If you’re short on time, make sure to use all your attacks by finding a suitable opponent, regardless of the priority order.

2. Clash of Worlds

  • On the first and second day of battle, attacks will be assigned by the Guild Leader and carried out in sequence.
  • Once all gates leading to the Citadel are cleared, or if attacking intermediate gates is deemed ineffective, the remaining attacks may proceed in a free-attack format based on priority.
  • If the opposing guild’s gates are too difficult to break through, free attacks may also be allowed.

3. Raids

  • Teams with lower CP are encouraged to attack on Friday (the first day raids open) to secure the 10M damage reward.
  • For advice on team composition or strategy, please use Guild Chat to ask questions and share information.

Raid Strategy: 150 Osh Efficiency

These guidelines outline the tactical delay requirements for high-damage teams to maximize our overall success against the Level 150 Osh boss. The following criteria regarding damage output take precedence over previous team-specific recommendations.

Target Members: These guidelines apply to all guild members who possess teams capable of consistently recording over 15M damage (achieving this threshold at least twice against the Level 150 Osh boss), or who possess an Isaac team.

  • Restricted Period: Friday of the Osh Raid week.
  • Required Action: Members meeting these criteria must delay their attacks with these specific teams (including Isaac teams) until the later stages of the Raid (post-Friday). Attacks with Isaac teams are delayed regardless of damage.
  • Additional Action: If the total cumulative damage from all your teams exceeds 10M, you are also required to delay any remaining attacks.
  • Exceptions: Attacks during the restricted period are only permitted if coordinated with and approved by the Guild Leadership (Officers) in advance.

4. Arena & Grand Arena

We aim to balance individual growth with guild harmony. To minimize friction while allowing for competitive progression, the following rules apply to both Arena and Grand Arena:

  • Competition Scope: These policies now explicitly cover both Arena and Grand Arena environments.
  • Rank-Based Attack Limit: You are prohibited from attacking guild members who are more than two ranks higher than your current position.
    • Example: If you are ranked 15th, you may attack a guild member up to the 13th rank. Attacking a guild member at the 12th rank or higher is prohibited.
  • Frequency Limit: You may not attack the same guild member more than twice per day.
  • Exception for Lower Ranks: These restrictions do not apply when attacking a guild member ranked lower than yourself.
    • Note: Since defensive logs do not display the attacker's rank, it can be unclear who is attacking from a higher or lower position. Members (especially Top 3 rankers) must prioritize direct communication to avoid misunderstandings.
  • Systematic Solution: We continue to encourage adjusting Arena reward time zones to de-sync windows and minimize direct conflict.
    • Related links: How to Change My Time Zone in the Game
    • Related links: A new day in Dominion

    ⚠️ Important Cautions:

    • Time zone changes can only be performed once every 30 days. Once changed, you cannot revert or modify it again for 30 days.
    • This change affects game modes and quest reset times. It may take up to 24 hours for game modes to sync and the reset time to update.

6. Area of Conquest (AoC)

To maximize our collective rewards and ensure fair participation, Guild XoX has established formal guidelines for the Area of Conquest event. All members are expected to contribute to the growth of our Castle.

The Goal of Cooperation in Area of Conquest

The primary objective in Area of Conquest is not just individual ranking, but the leveling up of the Guild Castle. Higher Castle levels provide more rewards for every single member. Therefore, hoarding coins or zero-contribution behavior hinders the progress of the entire guild.

Contribution Standards

We have established two tiers of contribution to ensure both participation and growth:

Category Minimum Coin Threshold Description & Expectation
Minimum Participation 5,000 Coins Easily achievable through quest rewards and collecting coins via combat and settlement capture.
Active Contributor 15,000+ Coins Our target for active growth. Members reaching this help the guild unlock higher-tier rewards.

Mandatory Daily Actions

  • Daily Combat: Engage in at least one fight per day (win or lose).
  • Settlement Management: Try to capture or hold at least one settlement to generate coins.
  • The Castle Rule: All collected coins MUST be invested into the Castle upgrades before the event ends.

7. Inactivity, Warnings, and Expulsion

  • Long-term inactivity without prior notice may result in removal from the guild without warning.
  • Failure to participate in guild activities will trigger notifications, and repeated warnings will lead to expulsion.
  • We will also inform members who record fewer than five attacks in the weekly Guild War statistics.
  • If you are unable to participate due to unavoidable circumstances (e.g., internet outages, national circumstances, personal emergencies), please inform the Guild Leader or Generals via PM or Guild Chat as soon as possible. Exemptions will be granted for documented issues.
  • New members who are expelled for inactivity during their initial period will be restricted from rejoining.
  • Members expelled for trolling in adventures will be restricted from rejoining.

Area of Conquest Participation Rules

We monitor individual contributions through the event log. The following rules apply to Area of Conquest:

  • Zero Contribution (0 Coins): Immediate "Yellow Card" warning. If no valid reason is provided (e.g., connection issues, personal emergency), a second occurrence will lead to immediate expulsion.
  • Repeated Low Contribution: Members who consistently stay below the 5,000 threshold without prior notice will be reviewed for potential removal.

8. Communication & Fair Play

As a guild, effective communication and fair play are essential for our success. To maintain a positive environment:

  • Actively share your progress, team compositions, and strategies in the guild chat to support fellow members.
  • During Arena competitions, prioritize respect and cooperation over aggressive rivalries to avoid conflicts.
  • If you have questions about any guidelines, including Raid timings, contact the Generals or Guild Leader promptly.

Final Note

Guild XoX values teamwork, respect, and communication. By following these guidelines, you help make our guild stronger and more enjoyable for everyone.
