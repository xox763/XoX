1. Raid Strategy: 150 Osh Efficiency

This policy outlines the tactical delay requirements for high-damage teams to maximize our overall success against the Level 150 Osh boss. The following criteria regarding damage output take precedence over previous team-specific recommendations.

Target Members: This policy applies to all guild members who possess teams capable of consistently recording over 15M damage (achieving this threshold at least twice consecutively against the Level 150 Osh boss).

  • Restricted Period: Friday of the Osh Raid week.
  • Required Action: Members meeting these criteria must delay their attacks with these specific teams until the later stages of the Raid (post-Friday).
  • Exceptions: Attacks during the restricted period are only permitted if coordinated with and approved by the Guild Leadership (Officers) in advance.

Strategic Alignment & Target Teams

While previous guidelines focused specifically on Isaac-based compositions, this updated policy prioritizes actual damage output regardless of team composition.

2. Arena: Inter-Guild Competition Policy

We aim to balance individual growth with guild harmony. After internal review, the following policy regarding Arena competition has been established:

  • No Prohibition: Attacks between guild members are not prohibited due to various tactical and progression reasons.
  • Priority on Communication: If persistent competition occurs between members, please prioritize direct communication and negotiation to reach a win-win compromise.
  • Systematic Solution: We are exploring the possibility of adjusting Arena reward time zones. I (the Guild Leader) will personally test time zone changes to see if we can de-sync reward windows and minimize direct conflict.

3. Communication & Fair Play

The strength of XoX lies in our ability to coordinate. Please remember:

  • Share your progress and team compositions in the guild chat.
  • Be respectful during Arena rivalries; a compromise is always better than a resource war.
  • Reach out to Generals or the Leader if you have questions regarding the new Raid timing.
